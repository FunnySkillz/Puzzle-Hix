# PuzzleDungeon Product Bible

## Direction

PuzzleDungeon is an Android-first hybrid-casual match-3 game with a cozy dungeon theme. The player clears readable magical tiles, unlocks a level-map path, earns stars and XP, collects treasure-themed cosmetics, and uses optional boosters for harder boards.

The game must feel original. It must not copy Candy Crush assets, names, branding, sounds, UI, levels, characters, or proprietary presentation.

## Audience

- Casual mobile players who want short sessions.
- Players who understand match-3 quickly but enjoy progression depth.
- Primary launch target: Android.
- Later target: iOS after Android validation.

## Core Loop

1. Open level map.
2. Pick an unlocked level.
3. Beat the objective within limited moves.
4. Earn stars, XP, coins, and occasional rewards.
5. Unlock the next level and improve old star results.
6. Return later for daily rewards, missions, and events.

## Visual Rules

- Cozy dungeon, treasure, relic, and magical-tile language.
- Readability first: each piece type must be distinguishable by shape/marking, not only color.
- Effects must clarify gameplay: swap, invalid swap, clear, cascade, special activation, win, fail.
- Store art and UI must be original and must not mimic an existing match-3 brand.

## Monetization Rules

- Budget remains 0 EUR until soft-launch readiness.
- Monetization is retention-first, not ad-spam.
- Rewarded ads are optional value exchanges only.
- Interstitial ads are disabled by default and can only be enabled later by remote config after retention is healthy.
- IAP v1 uses direct products: remove ads, coins, booster bundles, starter pack, and cosmetic bundles.
- Paid random loot boxes are not part of v1.
- Skins are cosmetic-only and must not provide gameplay advantage.
- Hard lives/energy are disabled for launch unless retention data later justifies testing them.

## Production Success Gates

- A new player understands level 1 without reading documentation.
- First 10 levels are beatable and readable on common portrait Android devices.
- Crash-free sessions reach at least 99% in test builds.
- Rewarded ad placements are optional and never block core progression.
- Progression can be completed through skill and patience without spending money.
